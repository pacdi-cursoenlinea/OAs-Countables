IMPORTANT CONSIDERATIONS

However the uncountable nouns can become countable if we use containers or phrases. Example:

  1. I always drink a lot of milk.
  2. She bought 2 cartons of milk.

You have to be careful when using the phrases to express quantities.  Some of the phrases are used with countable nouns and some others are used with uncountable nouns. It is also important to check if the sentence is affirmative, negative or a question.
